I'm Leon Dupree — born and based in Sevenoaks, and properly obsessed with computers since I was a kid. The kind who took things apart to see how they worked, then put them back together.

I started out as a junior data technician at Ramac Holdings here in Sevenoaks, where I spent three years handling everything from spreadsheets to a full digital filing migration. From there I went on to an IT technician internship at ADM Computing — diagnosing real hardware, software and network problems for real users.

Now I'm in the final year of a BSc Computing degree at Canterbury Christ Church University, after finishing my Extended Diploma in Computer Science with a Distinction* (the highest possible grade). Alongside my studies I'm building this — a tech support service that treats every job, big or small, like it actually matters.
